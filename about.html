To figure out what kind of life insurance you need, just answer these two questions:

1. Do I need life insurance? Which kind: whole or term?



2. How much insurance do I need?

Take your # of dependents, figure out how much $ they need per year, and multiply by the number of years they'll need to be supported. Don't forget about college!
